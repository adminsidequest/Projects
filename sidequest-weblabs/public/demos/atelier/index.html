04 What we do

Services

From the first sketch to the final handover, we stay on the drawing through every decision.

  • 01

    Architecture

    New build homes, extensions, and adaptive reuse. Concept through planning, technical drawings, and on site delivery.

  • 02

    Interiors

    Spatial planning, joinery, lighting, and material palettes. We treat the inside with the same rigour as the shell.

  • 03

    Master Planning

    Site strategy for clusters, campuses, and public realm. Phasing, density, and movement studied at scale.

  • 04

    Heritage and Reuse

    Sensitive work to listed and characterful buildings. We keep what matters and let the new read clearly against it.

  • 05

    Furniture and Detail

    Bespoke pieces and fittings designed for the room they sit in. Drawn, prototyped, and made with trusted workshops.

05 The Studio

A small team that
draws everything
itself.

Atelier is twelve architects and designers working from a converted print works in east London. We keep the studio small on purpose, so the people who win the work are the same people who draw it.

We believe a good building is mostly subtraction. We remove until only what is needed remains, then we detail that with care. The result reads as calm, but every line has been argued for.
