    The place

    A small house on a long, forgotten stretch of coast.

    Aurelia began as a fisherman's cottage and grew, slowly, the way coastal things do. We kept the lime washed walls, the worn teak, the smell of the garden after rain. Everything else we built around one idea, that rest should feel like remembering something you forgot you knew.

    Mornings are for swimming before anyone else wakes. Afternoons drift. Evenings end on the terrace, with the sea going quiet and a glass of something cold in your hand.
