The Collection

Considered, never ornamental

A small, edited line. Each piece is cast in recycled 14k and 18k gold, finished by a single maker, and stamped with our mark.

The Craft

Made slowly, by one pair of hands

Every Lustre piece begins as a lost wax carving and ends at a polishing wheel. We work in recycled gold and pair it with stones traced to responsible mines, so the metal on your hand carries no hidden cost.

Nothing here is mass produced. A ring takes roughly nine days to make, and we would not have it any other way.
