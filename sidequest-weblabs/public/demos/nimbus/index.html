Features

Everything you need, nothing you do not

Six focused tools that replace the spreadsheet sprawl. Built for clarity, tuned for speed.

Live dashboards

Build a board in minutes with drag and drop tiles. Numbers refresh on their own, so you never present stale data.

Anomaly alerts

Nimbus learns your normal range and pings you in Slack or email the moment a metric drifts off course.

One click reports

Turn any view into a polished weekly summary. Schedule it once and the recap lands in inboxes every Monday.

Shared workspaces

Invite the whole team with granular roles. Comment on a chart, leave context, and keep decisions next to the data.

50+ integrations

Connect your warehouse, product analytics, billing, and CRM. Nimbus stitches the sources into one tidy model.

Enterprise security

SOC 2 Type II, SSO, and row level permissions come standard. Your numbers stay yours, encrypted at rest and in flight.

How it works

Live in three steps, not three weeks

No data team required. Connect a source and Nimbus does the heavy lifting.

  1. 1

    Connect your sources

    Authenticate your tools in a few clicks. Nimbus maps the fields and starts syncing within minutes.

  2. 2

    Pick your metrics

    Choose from a curated library or define your own. Drag tiles onto a board and arrange the story you want to tell.

  3. 3

    Share the insight

    Invite the team, schedule a recap, and let alerts keep everyone honest. Decisions get faster from day one.
